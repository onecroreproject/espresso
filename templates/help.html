Common Questions

How can I track my order? expand_more
You can track your order in the "My Orders" section of your profile. We'll also send you push notifications for status updates.
What are the reward points? expand_more
You earn points for every purchase. These points can be redeemed for discounts, free coffee, and exclusive rewards.
How do I top up my wallet? expand_more
Go to the Wallet section and click "Top Up". You can use any saved payment method or a new credit card.

Need more help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions.
